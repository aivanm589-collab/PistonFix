Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

PistonFix ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our vehicle maintenance tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address and name when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Vehicle data: Information you enter about your vehicles including make, model, year, mileage, and license plates.
  • Maintenance records: Service history, costs, parts, and notes you enter for your vehicles.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the PistonFix service
  • Store your vehicle maintenance records securely
  • Send maintenance reminders you configure
  • Improve and optimize the application
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using industry-standard encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Supabase (our database provider) for data storage
  • Google (for authentication when you use Sign in with Google)
  • When required by law

5. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. We do not have access to your Google password or other Google account data. Our use of Google user data complies with Google's API Services User Data Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data (available via PDF/Excel export in the app)

8. Children's Privacy

PistonFix is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us
